Do you want to hear your voice on the radio?

There are three types of voice-over we like on the radio:

Transitions
Play roughly every four hours.
Album intros
Play before an album.
Example: "Next up, the Neotokyo soundtrack."
Track intros
Play before a track.
Example: "The next track is Skull, by GosT."

It doesn't have to be your voice, it can be distorted or synthesised, or whatever. A transition could even be a soundbite from something.

Between 15 and 30 seconds is a good length.
